Quinn — what most service members don't know

If you have a child or spouse, you can transfer up to 36 months of unused GI Bill to them under TEB — but the transfer has to happen while you're still on active duty, and the rules require an additional 4-year service obligation from the date of approval. Maj. Chen can walk you through the math during your call — she's transferred her own benefit, knows the wrinkles.
